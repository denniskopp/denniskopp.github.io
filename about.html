About

Hello, I'm Dennis.

Freelance Translator | English & French → German

I’m a freelance translator based in Essen, Germany, specializing in translation from English and French into German. My goal is to help businesses communicate clearly and naturally with German-speaking audiences through accurate and well-crafted translations.

My professional background combines language expertise with international business experience. I completed a three-year training program as a state-certified foreign language assistant, followed by a three-year apprenticeship in international trade management. During this time, I regularly communicated with English and French-speaking clients and developed a strong understanding of international business communication.

Languages and cultures have always fascinated me. I enjoy traveling, spending time in nature and discovering new perspectives through language. In my free time, I follow football and enjoy video games.
